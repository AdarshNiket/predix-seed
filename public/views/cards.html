A Card is an editable container that is composed by the application developer and resides within a larger container we call a View. A View is simply the background area of the screen where your content is displayed. The white area on this page is the view. The individual cards are the sections of content that sit within the view. In this section cards will be shown with these double ended arrows.

A Deck is comprised of a series of Cards that are organized by you, the developer. The Deck enables card to card communication.
